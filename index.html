iPad Pro 12.9

The iPad Pro family is a line of iPad tablet computers designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc., that runs the iOS and iPadOS mobile operating systems. Its current generation is available in two screen sizes, 11-inch and 12.9-inch, each with four options for internal storage capacities: 64, 256, 512, and 1024 GB.
